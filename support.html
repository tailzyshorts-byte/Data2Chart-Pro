FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Does Data2Chart-Pro store my CSV files?
No. All data processing happens locally inside your browser and Canva environment. We have no servers that receive or store your data.
Why isn't my CSV being parsed correctly?
Make sure your CSV uses standard comma-separated formatting with a header row in the first line. Complex multi-header or merged-cell structures may not be supported. When contacting support, attach a sample of your CSV for faster help.
Who owns the charts I create?
You do. Charts generated by you using Data2Chart-Pro within Canva belong to you, subject to Canva's own licensing terms.
How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Email us at support@data2chartpro.com or use the support link on our Canva App listing. Include as much detail as possible, including steps to reproduce the issue.
